Final Project
Ryan Steed and Xavier Boudreau

Boudreau:
Coded mechanics of game, including deck creation and shuffling and the calling of cards with specific attributes denoting suit, value and symbol, as well as gameplay regarding actions and outcomes. 

Steed:
Coded asthetics of game, including HTML and CSS structure and style, and on-screen interaction/movement (alerts, card pictures, music).


Supported in Mozilla Firefox; not fully supported (beyond first round) in Chrome and Safari due to "use strict"    ; javascript requirement.
